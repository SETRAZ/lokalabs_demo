LivNow


Chewing gum is an age-old practice with numerous health benefits. The modern lifestyle and eating habits reduced the chewing habit leading to weak gums and teeth. The reduced chewing combined with intake of processed sugar led to oral cavity problems including tooth cavities, plaque formation, bleeding gums, tooth sensitivity, infections, among others. Chewing a simple gum can provide benefits attributed to increased saliva secretion that will clear unwanted materials in the oral cavity. Chewing gum would also strengthen the jaws and facial muscles. However, a simple flavoured gum is now a thing of past.

The present and future of chewing gum is to utilize the product for direct health benefits. The normal chewing time is expected to be from 10-60 min. This is a sufficient time for any nutrient or drug present in chewing gum to provide local action within the oral cavity or systemic action, where the active compounds get rapidly absorbed through the buccal mucosa.

LivNow is a premium chewing gum product that offers varied health benefits. The natural active ingredients drawn from Indian Ayurvedic system provide overall wellbeing to human body and mind.
