Microdiagnostics


Microneedle device is a revolutionary technology to deliver drugs into the skin and detect biomolecules in the skin in a pain-free manner. Microneedles are an array of tiny needles that are 400-1000 µm long with the tip diameter as small as 20 µm.

We make polymer-based microneedle devices that are biocompatible and biodegradable. The microneedle patch can be self-administered without the need for a medical doctor. Microneedle patch can be applied on the forearm with a simple finger press. The pain-free application of microneedle patch for 5 minutes can allow you to record biomolecule concentration within the interstitial fluid of the skin. The device can provide real-time measurement of glucose without the need for finger pricking and drawing blood with the same accuracy.

The microneedle device is currently in the development and validation phase. The device is studied for its performance in animal models and tested in a few human volunteers that showed remarkable results.

The microneedle device is being fabricated by 3D printing technology and micromolding techniques.
